FAQ

I try to post once a week, but that may fluctuate due to my job. The current schedule is the following: Fridays at noon, EST

Will we ever get a face reveal?

I don't want to share a lot about my physical appearance because I don't want it to affect the way people listen to my audios.

What kind of mic do you use?

I use an AKG P220 mic with a wrap-around pop filter and shock mount on a table mounted boom.

What do you use for editing?

I use Audacity with RX 7 plugins as well as Reaper, Nectar 3, Nutron 3, Ozone 9, and occasionally some other plugins.
